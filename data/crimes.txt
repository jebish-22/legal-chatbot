🔹crime & Safety


House Robbery / Burglary – IPC 379, 380, 454, CrPC 154

Mobile Theft / Snatching – IPC 379, 356

Car/Bike Theft – IPC 379, 411 (dishonestly receiving stolen property)

Assault / Physical Attack – IPC 323 (hurt), 325 (grievous hurt), 506 (criminal intimidation)

Chain Snatching / Street Robbery – IPC 392, 356

Sexual Harassment / Eve-Teasing – IPC 354A, 509

Rape / Sexual Assault – IPC 375, 376

Domestic Violence – IPC 498A, Domestic Violence Act 2005

Dowry Harassment – IPC 498A, Dowry Prohibition Act 1961

Kidnapping / Missing Person – IPC 361, 363, 364


🔹 Cyber & Technology


Online Banking Fraud / UPI Scam – IT Act Sec 66C, 66D, IPC 420

Phishing / Email Scam – IT Act Sec 66D, IPC 419

Identity Theft (fake account, misuse of Aadhaar/PAN) – IT Act 66C, IPC 419/468

Online Defamation / Harassment – IPC 499, 500; IT Act 66A* (struck down, but police still cite), use IPC 354D (stalking)

Cyberbullying / Online Stalking – IPC 354D, IT Act 66E (violation of privacy)


🔹 Property & Civil


Land / Property Dispute (trespass, encroachment) – IPC 441 (criminal trespass), 447

Illegal Eviction by Landlord – IPC 441, Rent Control Acts (state-specific), CrPC 145

Cheque Bounce – NI Act Sec 138

Fraudulent Documents / Forgery – IPC 467, 468, 471

Bribery / Corruption Complaint – PCA (Prevention of Corruption Act), IPC 171E

🔹 Accidents & Emergencies


Road Accident (injury) – IPC 279, 337, 338; MV Act Sec 134

Road Accident (death) – IPC 304A

Hit-and-Run Case – IPC 304A, 279; MV Act Sec 134

Fire / Arson – IPC 435, 436

Medical Negligence – IPC 304A, Consumer Protection Act


🔹 Family & Social


Child Abuse / Child Labour – JJ Act 2015, IPC 75, 370

Elder Abuse / Neglect – Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act 2007

Marriage Fraud (fake promises, NRI fraud) – IPC 417, 420

Workplace Harassment (POSH Act) – IPC 354A, POSH Act 2013

Police Harassment / Refusal to File FIR – CrPC 154(3), CrPC 156(3)
